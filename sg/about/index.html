Our Mission

PostFre Singapore was created with one simple goal, to give everyone in Singapore an easy, fast, and completely free platform to post and browse listings.
From property to jobs, services, and items for sale, we believe classifieds should always be accessible without fees or restrictions.

What We Offer

  • ✔ Free classifieds for property, jobs, services, and buy or sell items
  • ✔ No account required to browse listings
  • ✔ No posting fees
  • ✔ Simple, clean, mobile-friendly browsing
  • ✔ Direct contact between listers and interested buyers

Why We Built This

Most platforms charge listing fees or limit visibility unless users pay.
PostFre Singapore offers a clean, fast, community-driven space where anyone can post and discover listings freely, without complicated steps or hidden costs.
