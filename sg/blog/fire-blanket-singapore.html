When to Use a Fire Blanket

  • Kitchen stove fires or cooking oil flare-ups
  • Small electrical appliance fires
  • Clothing fires (wrap safely to extinguish)
  • Small fires in homes, shops or offices

A properly installed fire blanket in Singapore can stop a fire within seconds, minimise property damage and prevent serious injuries. Fire blankets require no training—just pull the tabs, cover the fire and allow it to cool.
