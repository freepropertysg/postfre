What Is SRX X-Value?

SRX X-Value is a popular automated valuation reference in Singapore. It estimates property value based on historical transactions, market trends and property characteristics.

Buyers, sellers, landlords and agents often use valuation tools like this to check whether a price is reasonable.

How Property Valuation Works in Singapore

Professional valuation models rely on large datasets. A simplified valuation can still be useful by using:

  • Estimated price per square foot (PSF)
  • Unit size in square feet
  • A reasonable market adjustment range

Why Use a Property Valuation Calculator?

A valuation calculator helps you assess whether an asking price or offer price makes sense.

  • Instant estimation within seconds
  • Useful for buyers, sellers and investors
  • Works for HDB, condo and landed homes
  • Helps avoid overpaying

What PSF Should You Use?

The most accurate PSF usually comes from recent transactions in the same project or nearby developments with similar characteristics.

Using a realistic PSF produces a more reliable valuation estimate.
