Posting Guidelines

These Posting Guidelines help keep PostFre useful, safe and easy to use for everyone. They explain what makes a good listing and why some listings may be removed.

What You Can Post

  • Genuine property, job, service or buy-and-sell listings
  • Accurate information about items, services or opportunities
  • Listings that comply with applicable laws and regulations

How to Create a Good Listing

  • Use a clear and honest title
  • Write a detailed and accurate description
  • Include correct pricing, location and contact details
  • Upload relevant and real images where possible

What Is Not Allowed

  • Misleading, false or fraudulent listings
  • Spam, duplicate or repetitive postings
  • Content that is illegal, harmful or offensive
  • Listings that violate applicable laws or regulations

Duplicate and Spam Listings

Posting the same or very similar listings multiple times may result in removal. Please update your existing listing instead of posting duplicates.

Removal of Listings

Listings that do not follow these Posting Guidelines or our Terms of Service may be removed at any time without prior notice.

Reporting a Listing

If you believe a listing violates these guidelines or our Terms, please contact us at admin@postfre.com.

Important Note

These Posting Guidelines are provided for general guidance only. They do not replace or override our Terms of Service.
