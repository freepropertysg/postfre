About PostFre

PostFre is derived from “post” and “fre” where “fre” represents freedom of participation — the idea of reducing friction in advertising by minimizing unnecessary restrictions on posting. Built as an open platform, PostFre connects buyers, sellers and service providers across regions, enabling broader reach through simpler posting. Platform rules and moderation exist only where necessary to safeguard all parties and support long-term sustainability, without compromising the flexibility and openness that define PostFre.

Our Mission

PostFre was created with one simple goal to provide an easy and accessible platform for posting and browsing classified listings.
From property and jobs to services and items for sale, we believe listings should be straightforward, open and easy to use.

What We Offer

  • ✔ Classified listings for property, jobs, services and items for sale
  • ✔ No account required to browse listings
  • ✔ Simple and straightforward listing process
  • ✔ Clean, mobile-friendly browsing experience
  • ✔ Direct contact between listers and interested users

Why We Built This

Many platforms introduce unnecessary complexity or restrictions for users.
PostFre focuses on simplicity, speed and transparency, making it easier to post and discover listings without unnecessary steps.
