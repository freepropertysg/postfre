About PostFre

PostFre is a coined brand name created to represent a simple and practical approach to online classifieds. It exists to make posting and finding listings easier, without unnecessary systems or complexity.

What PostFre Is

PostFre operates as a curated classified platform that bridges buyers and sellers. It provides a space where buyers and sellers can connect directly.

How Posting Works

Placing an advertisement on PostFre is simple.

To submit a listing, users email their advertisement details to info@postfre.com

All submissions are manually reviewed before being published. This helps maintain listing quality, reduce spam and keep the platform useful for everyone.

The Role of PostFre

PostFre acts as a publishing platform only. It does not participate in negotiations, transactions or agreements between users.

All communication and transactions take place directly between buyers and sellers.

Growing the Platform

We are continuously working to bring more traffic and visibility to the platform so listings receive wider reach over time.

If you find PostFre useful, we welcome your support. Sharing the platform within your social circle helps grow the community, allowing more buyers and sellers to connect through a simple, open classifieds space.

Our Focus

PostFre is built with a long-term mindset: clarity over volume, simplicity over automation and usefulness over noise.

It exists to make posting straightforward and finding listings easy.
