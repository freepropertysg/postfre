About PostFre

PostFre is derived from “post” and “fre”, where “fre” represents freedom. Freedom from spending countless hours promoting products and services. Freedom from managing multiple platforms, tools and campaigns. Freedom for business owners and service providers to focus on improving what they offer, while visibility is handled for them.

What PostFre Is

PostFre is a centralized promotion platform designed for businesses and service providers who want reach without complexity. Instead of requiring users to learn marketing systems or manage advertising efforts, PostFre provides a single, streamlined entry point for promotion.

How PostFre Works

Businesses and service providers simply email us with their campaign details — including their product or service, target audience and marketing objective.

From there, PostFre takes care of the rest, placing and positioning the promotion across the PostFre.com platform to deliver maximum reach and visibility.

The process is simple. The execution is handled. The focus stays on the business.

Why PostFre Exists

Modern promotion often demands constant attention — content creation, optimisation, platform management and ongoing adjustments. PostFre exists to remove that burden.

We believe promotion should function as quiet infrastructure, not a daily responsibility. “Fre” reflects this principle — the freedom to operate, improve and grow, while promotion is managed with intent and clarity.

Who PostFre Is For

  • ✔ Business owners
  • ✔ Service providers
  • ✔ Independent professionals
  • ✔ Growing brands seeking efficient visibility

The PostFre Approach

PostFre is intentionally focused. It is built to do one thing well — simplify promotion for businesses that value time, clarity and control.
